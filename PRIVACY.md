# Privacy Policy

**Gmail Email Rewriter** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

## How It Works

- All email processing happens **entirely on your device** using an in-browser AI model via WebGPU.
- Email content is **never sent** to any external server, API, or third party.
- Rewrite results are cached **locally** in your browser's extension storage and never leave your machine.

## Data Access

The extension accesses email content (subject and body text) solely to provide rewrite suggestions displayed inline in Gmail. This data is processed locally and is not logged, collected, or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

## Changes

If this policy changes, updates will be posted here.
